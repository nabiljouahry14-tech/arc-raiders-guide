Strategic Maps

Master every location on the ravaged surface

Dam Battlegrounds

High Difficulty Large Map

A massive hydroelectric dam complex with multi-level combat zones. Features open courtyards, narrow corridors, and vertical gameplay opportunities.

Advantages

  • Multiple extraction points for tactical flexibility
  • High-tier loot in control tower and surveillance rooms
  • Excellent sniping positions on dam walls
  • Underground tunnels provide escape routes
  • Water channels can be used for stealth movement

Disadvantages

  • Heavy ARC presence, especially Titans and Enforcers
  • Limited cover in main courtyard areas
  • Bottleneck chokepoints easily defended
  • Long travel distances between key locations
  • Turbine rooms have poor visibility

Key Locations

  • Control Tower: High-value loot, Dam Control Tower Key required
  • Surveillance Room: Electronic components, special access card needed
  • Turbine Hall: Industrial materials and ARC parts
  • Worker's Quarters: Medical supplies and weapons
  • Spillway: Extraction point with moderate risk

Pro Tips

  • Clear the control tower early for tactical advantage
  • Use the dam walls for long-range reconnaissance
  • Bring EMP weapons for the heavy ARC concentration
  • The underwater passages are dangerous but rewarding

Buried City

Medium Difficulty Large Map

Ancient Italian ruins half-buried in sand and debris. A maze of buildings, streets, and underground catacombs create complex urban warfare scenarios.

Advantages

  • Abundant cover and hiding spots throughout
  • Rich historical artifacts and rare collectibles
  • Multiple entry and exit points to every building
  • Vertical gameplay with rooftop access
  • Catacombs provide safe passage between areas

Disadvantages

  • Easy to get disoriented in the ruins
  • Numerous ambush points favor defenders
  • Close-quarters combat favors shotguns and SMGs
  • Collapsing structures pose environmental hazards
  • Seeker ARCs patrol extensively in tight spaces

Key Locations

  • Cathedral Square: Central hub with high loot density
  • Museum: Rare artifacts and historical items
  • Underground Market: Trading post remnants with supplies
  • Bell Tower: Best vantage point on the map
  • Ancient Catacombs: Secret passages and hidden caches

Pro Tips

  • Learn the catacomb network for quick escapes
  • The bell tower offers 360-degree views
  • Listen for footsteps in narrow streets
  • Avoid the main square during high activity

Spaceport

Extreme Difficulty Extra Large Map

Massive abandoned space infrastructure with launch pads, hangars, and research facilities. The most dangerous and rewarding location.

Advantages

  • Highest tier loot available anywhere
  • Advanced technology and rare blueprints
  • Massive open areas for vehicle use
  • Research labs contain unique items
  • Launch facilities have legendary caches

Disadvantages

  • Extremely high ARC concentration including bosses
  • Open areas leave raiders exposed
  • Long distances between cover points
  • Radiation zones in damaged reactor areas
  • Frequent Raider squad conflicts

Key Locations

  • Launch Control: Command center with elite loot
  • Research Complex: Experimental weapons and tech
  • Hangar Bay Alpha: Vehicle parts and fuel
  • Reactor Core: High-risk, high-reward legendary items
  • Observation Deck: Strategic vantage point

Pro Tips

  • Full squad mandatory - never go solo
  • Bring radiation suits for reactor area
  • Use vehicles to cross open ground quickly
  • Boss ARCs spawn here - be prepared

The Blue Gate

Low Difficulty Medium Map

A small fortified settlement with residential areas and market districts. Ideal for new Raiders learning the extraction mechanics.

Advantages

  • Beginner-friendly with clear landmarks
  • Moderate loot with consistent quality
  • Fewer ARCs and lower threat levels
  • Close extraction points
  • Good for solo play

Disadvantages

  • Lower quality loot overall
  • Popular spot means more Raider conflicts
  • Limited strategic depth
  • Predictable layout favors experienced players
  • Fewer unique items

Key Locations

  • Market Square: Basic supplies and consumables
  • Blue Gate Cellar: Hidden stash, key required
  • Guard Tower: Weapons and ammunition
  • Residential Block: Medical supplies
  • Town Hall: Maps and intel

Pro Tips

  • Perfect for practicing extraction timing
  • Check the cellar for hidden treasures
  • Use as warmup before harder maps
  • Great for testing new loadouts

Stella Montis

High Difficulty Large Map

Mountainous terrain with ski resort ruins, mining facilities, and observation posts. Vertical gameplay at its finest with elevation advantages.

Advantages

  • Elevation provides tactical advantages
  • Mining facilities have rare ore and materials
  • Cable cars offer unique movement options
  • Observatory has excellent sightlines
  • Winter conditions reduce ARC sensors

Disadvantages

  • Harsh weather effects reduce visibility
  • Avalanche zones are extremely dangerous
  • Limited cover on mountain slopes
  • Cold weather drains stamina faster
  • Falls from heights are lethal

Key Locations

  • Summit Observatory: Best vantage point, rare loot
  • Mining Complex: Rare minerals and ores
  • Ski Lodge: Supplies and equipment
  • Cable Car Station: Quick transport and gear
  • Mountain Cave: Hidden stashes and shelter

Pro Tips

  • Use cable cars strategically but expect ambushes
  • Bring warm gear for extended stays
  • High ground is crucial - control the summit
  • Watch for avalanche warning signs
