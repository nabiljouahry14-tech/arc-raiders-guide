Getting Started

What is ARC Raiders?

ARC Raiders is a multiplayer extraction shooter set in a post-apocalyptic future Earth. Your goal is to venture onto the dangerous surface, collect valuable loot, and extract safely before time runs out or you're eliminated by ARCs or other Raiders.

Your First Match

  1. Choose Your Map: Start with "The Blue Gate" - it's the most beginner-friendly location with lower ARC density.
  2. Select Your Loadout: Begin with the basic assault rifle and pistol. Simple but effective.
  3. Learn the Layout: Spend your first few matches exploring and learning landmark locations.
  4. Loot Carefully: Don't be greedy. Take what you need and head to extraction.
  5. Extract Successfully: Reach an extraction point and survive the timer to keep your loot.

Core Game Mechanics

Match Timer

Each match has a 20-minute timer. When time runs out, a deadly storm closes in, forcing all players to extraction points. Plan your looting route accordingly.

Health System

Your health is divided into segments. Damage depletes these segments, which can only be restored with medical items. Learn to heal strategically during safe moments.

Stamina Management

Sprinting, jumping, and climbing consume stamina. Running out of stamina leaves you vulnerable. Manage it carefully during combat and exploration.

Inventory System

Limited inventory space means you must prioritize valuable loot. Backpacks increase capacity but are rare. Decide what's worth keeping.

Armor System

Armor protects you from damage but degrades with use. Always carry spare plates or repair kits. Higher tier armor provides better protection.

Sound Design

Footsteps, gunfire, and ARC sounds are crucial. Use headphones. Listen for audio cues to detect enemies and threats before seeing them.

Combat Tips & Tactics

Against ARCs

  • Always aim for weak points (glowing sections)
  • Use cover extensively - ARCs have aimbot-level accuracy
  • EMP weapons disable ARCs temporarily
  • Stealth kills on Scout ARCs prevent alerts
  • Heavy ARCs require team focus fire
  • Learn ARC patrol patterns to avoid unnecessary fights
  • Explosives deal massive damage to armored units

Against Other Raiders

  • Listen for footsteps and gunfire
  • High ground advantage is crucial
  • Use grenades to flush out campers
  • Strafe during gunfights to avoid shots
  • Communicate with your squad constantly
  • Choose your battles - not every fight is worth it
  • Third-party fights when possible

Engagement Strategies

Aggressive Push

When: You have superior numbers or position

How: Use smokes/flashbangs, push together, overwhelm with firepower

Risk: High - can backfire if ambushed

Defensive Hold

When: You're outnumbered or in a strong position

How: Use cover, set up crossfires, force enemies to push

Risk: Medium - can get flanked

Tactical Retreat

When: Fight isn't going well or storm approaching

How: Suppress enemies, use smokes, reposition strategically

Risk: Low - survival is priority

Extraction Guide

Understanding Extraction

Extraction is the only way to keep your looted items. Each map has multiple extraction points, but they come with risks.

Safe Extractions

  • Always available
  • 60-second timer
  • Heavily contested by other Raiders
  • Examples: Main Extraction, Emergency Evac

Risky Extractions

  • Sometimes available (check timer)
  • 30-second timer
  • Less contested but unpredictable
  • Examples: Side Routes, Hidden Paths

Premium Extractions

  • Requires payment or special item
  • 15-second timer
  • Rarely contested
  • Examples: Helicopter, Private Vehicle

Extraction Survival Tips

  1. Clear the Area: Check for campers before committing to extraction
  2. Watch Your Back: Face away from the extraction point to see approaching threats
  3. Use Cover: Stay in cover during the timer countdown
  4. Team Coordination: Have squadmates watch different angles
  5. Backup Plans: Always know where alternative extractions are
  6. Timing Matters: Late extractions (15-20 min) are safer but riskier due to storm

Advanced Tactics

Sound Management

Master audio to gain the upper hand:

  • Walk instead of run in contested areas
  • Close doors behind you to hear intruders
  • Use directional audio to pinpoint enemies
  • Use suppressed weapons when sniping

Map Knowledge Exploitation

  • Learn all loot spawn locations
  • Memorize efficient looting routes
  • Know common camping spots
  • Use shortcuts and hidden passages
  • Control high-ground positions

Economy Management

  • Don't risk expensive loadouts on risky runs
  • Balance risk versus reward
  • Build your stash gradually
  • Reinvest profits into better gear
  • Keep a safety fund for bad streaks

Team Coordination

  • Constant callouts - enemy positions, loot, threats
  • Assign roles (point man, support, sniper)
  • Share loot based on loadout needs
  • Never split up unless absolutely necessary
  • Revive teammates - four guns better than two
