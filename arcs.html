Know your enemy. The mechanized threat explained.

The ARC Threat

The ARCs are mysterious mechanized entities that ravage the Earth's surface. These machines come in various forms, each with unique abilities and threat levels. Understanding their patterns, weaknesses, and tactics is essential for survival.

Low

Minor threat, can be handled solo

Medium

Moderate threat, team recommended

High

Severe threat, full squad required

Extreme

Deadly threat, avoid if possible

S-Tier - Elite Threats

Extreme Threat

Bison

Armor: Maximum
Speed: Medium
Damage: Extreme

Description: Massive heavily-armored quadruped ARC with devastating area attacks. One of the most dangerous ARCs encountered.

Weaknesses: Exposed power core after charge attacks, vulnerable legs during recovery phase

Tactics: Full squad essential. Dodge charge attacks, target legs to stagger, focus fire on core when exposed.

Loot: Legendary components, heavy plating, epic weapons

Extreme Threat

The Queen

Armor: Very Heavy
Speed: Fast
Damage: Extreme

Description: Aerial superiority ARC with advanced mobility and devastating ranged attacks. Commands battlefield from above.

Weaknesses: Energy vents when hovering, vulnerable during landing sequences

Tactics: Use high ground. Bring anti-air weapons. Coordinate attacks when it lands to recharge.

Loot: Flight systems, rare energy cells, legendary materials

Extreme Threat

Rocketeer

Armor: Heavy
Speed: Medium
Damage: Extreme

Description: Long-range artillery ARC equipped with devastating rocket barrages. Provides fire support from a distance.

Weaknesses: Long reload times, exposed missile pods, weak armor on sides

Tactics: Close distance quickly. Use cover from rocket barrages. Attack during reload phases.

Loot: Explosive components, targeting systems, advanced electronics

A-Tier - Advanced Units

High Threat

Bastion

Armor: Very Heavy
Speed: Slow
Damage: High

Description: Defensive fortress ARC with deployable shields and suppressive fire capabilities. Acts as mobile cover for other ARCs.

Weaknesses: Rear armor plates, overheats shield generators, slow rotation speed

Tactics: Flank from behind. Use EMP to disable shields. Focus fire when shield is down.

Loot: Shield generators, heavy plating, defensive systems

High Threat

Fireball

Armor: Medium
Speed: Fast
Damage: Very High

Description: Incendiary specialist ARC that deploys fire-based area denial. Creates hazardous zones that damage over time.

Weaknesses: Exposed fuel tanks, vulnerable to explosions, combustible when damaged

Tactics: Engage from range. Target fuel tanks for chain explosions. Avoid close quarters.

Loot: Incendiary components, thermal systems, fire-resistant materials

High Threat

Wasp

Armor: Light
Speed: Very Fast
Damage: Medium

Description: Fast aerial scout that harasses from above with hit-and-run attacks. Highly mobile and difficult to track.

Weaknesses: Low armor, predictable flight patterns, vulnerable during attack dives

Tactics: Lead shots for moving targets. Use automatic weapons. Take cover during dive attacks.

Loot: Flight components, sensors, lightweight alloys

B-Tier - Standard Units

Medium Threat

Sentinel

Armor: Heavy
Speed: Slow
Damage: Medium

Description: Static defensive ARC with long-range scanning and area suppression. Guards key locations and routes.

Weaknesses: Limited mobility, blind spots at close range, exposed sensors

Tactics: Approach from flanks. Use stealth to avoid detection. Disable sensors first.

Loot: Surveillance equipment, sensor arrays, targeting systems

Medium Threat

Hornet

Armor: Light
Speed: Fast
Damage: Medium

Description: Swarm-type aerial drone that attacks in coordinated groups. Individually weak but deadly in numbers.

Weaknesses: Fragile armor, loses coordination when separated, vulnerable to AOE

Tactics: Use area damage weapons. Prioritize swarm reduction. Keep moving to avoid encirclement.

Loot: Drone components, small electronics, scrap materials

C-Tier - Common Units

Low Threat

Tick

Armor: Light
Speed: Fast
Damage: Low

Description: Small spider-like scout ARC that detects and marks Raiders. Alerts nearby ARCs to presence.

Weaknesses: Minimal armor, fragile legs, low health pool

Tactics: Eliminate quickly and quietly. Use melee or suppressed weapons. Prevent alerting other ARCs.

Loot: Sensors, light materials, basic components

Low Threat

Rollbot

Armor: Medium
Speed: Medium
Damage: Low

Description: Spherical rolling ARC used for reconnaissance and light patrol. Can quickly traverse terrain.

Weaknesses: Exposed core when stationary, predictable rolling patterns, vulnerable to EMPs

Tactics: Attack when stationary. Use obstacles to block movement. Easy target for most weapons.

Loot: Mobility components, gyroscopes, scrap metal

Medium Threat

Pop

Armor: Light
Speed: Fast
Damage: Medium

Description: Explosive kamikaze ARC that rushes targets and detonates. Dangerous in groups.

Weaknesses: Explodes when destroyed, predictable charge pattern, fragile

Tactics: Destroy from distance. Use explosive chain reactions. Never let them get close.

Loot: Explosive materials, detonators, volatile compounds

Low Threat

Snitch

Armor: Minimal
Speed: Fast
Damage: None

Description: Non-combat surveillance drone that broadcasts Raider locations. Priority elimination target.

Weaknesses: Zero offensive capability, extremely fragile, slow reaction time

Tactics: Destroy immediately on sight. One shot kill. Critical to maintain stealth.

Loot: Surveillance tech, communication modules, signal equipment

D-Tier - Basic Units

Low Threat

Turret

Armor: Medium
Speed: None (Static)
Damage: Low

Description: Stationary defensive emplacement with limited range and field of view. Guards specific zones.

Weaknesses: Cannot move, limited rotation, exposed rear power source, blind spots

Tactics: Approach from dead angles. Destroy from cover. Easiest ARC to defeat with planning.

Loot: Basic components, ammunition, metal scrap

World Boss

World Boss

Matriarch

Armor: Maximum
Speed: Variable
Damage: Catastrophic

Description: The Matriarch is the ultimate ARC threat - a colossal biomechanical entity that serves as the supreme commander of all ARC forces. This massive spider-like boss features multiple weapon systems, armor plating, and the ability to summon reinforcements mid-combat. Encounters with the Matriarch are rare but represent the greatest challenge Raiders can face.

Weaknesses: Vulnerable leg joints after charge attacks, exposed power core during overcharge sequence, susceptible to coordinated heavy weapons fire, weak underbelly when stunned

Tactics: Minimum 6-8 player raid team required. Assign roles: damage dealers focus legs, support manages adds, heavy weapons target core. Use terrain for cover during artillery barrages. Coordinate burst damage during vulnerability windows. Bring EMP grenades to disrupt reinforcement calls. Stock up on anti-armor ammunition and repair kits.

Loot: Matriarch Core (unique material), Legendary weapon blueprints, Exotic armor pieces, Massive resource caches, Exclusive cosmetic items, Boss-specific mods and attachments

Spawns at designated world boss locations during timed events - requires extensive preparation and coordinated raid groups

General Combat Tips

Target Weak Points

Always aim for exposed joints, vents, and optical sensors for maximum damage.

Team Coordination

Focus fire on single targets and communicate enemy positions constantly.

Use EMP Effectively

EMP weapons temporarily disable ARCs, creating critical opportunities.

Know When to Retreat

Extraction is more important than engagement. Live to fight another day.
