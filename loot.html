Everything you need to know about resources and crafting

Rarity Tiers

Understanding loot quality and value

Common

Basic items found everywhere. Essential for early game progression.

Drop Rate: 60%

Uncommon

Improved quality with better stats. Common in mid-tier areas.

Drop Rate: 25%

Rare

High-quality items with special properties. Found in dangerous zones.

Drop Rate: 10%

Epic

Superior items with unique abilities. Elite enemy drops.

Drop Rate: 4%

Legendary

The best items in the game. Boss drops and special locations.

Drop Rate: 1%

Workshop Crafting Stations

Upgrade stations to unlock advanced recipes

Workbench

Basic crafting for weapons and ammo

  • Level 1: Basic weapons, all ammo types, shields, augments
  • Free Station: Available after 1 round played
  • Crafts: Ferro, Kettle, Rattler, Stitcher, Light/Medium/Heavy/Shotgun Ammo

Gunsmith

Weapon blueprints and modifications

  • Level 1: Basic weapon mods (grips, stocks, compensators)
  • Level 2: Arpeggio weapon + advanced mods
  • Level 3: Bettina, Renegade, Hullcracker + epic mods
  • Upgrade: 20× Metal + 30× Rubber → 3× Rusted Tools + 5× Mechanical + 8× Wasp Driver → 3× Rusted Gear + 5× Advanced Mechanical + 4× Sentinel Core

Gear Bench

Shields and augments crafting

  • Level 1: Light/Medium shields, Mk. 1 augments
  • Level 2: Heavy shields, Mk. 2 augments
  • Level 3: Mk. 3 augments (Aggressive, Flanking, Cautious, Survivor, Defensive, Healing)
  • Upgrade: 25× Plastic + 30× Fabric → 3× Power Cable + 5× Electrical + 5× Hornet Driver → 3× Industrial Battery + 5× Advanced Electrical + 6× Bastion Cell

Medical Lab

Healing items and shield rechargers

  • Level 1: Herbal bandage, shield recharger, adrenaline shot, bandage
  • Level 2: Sterilized bandage, surge shield recharger
  • Level 3: Vita spray, vita shot
  • Upgrade: 50× Fabric + 6× ARC Alloy → 2× Cracked Bioscanner + 5× Durable Cloth + 8× Tick Pod → 3× Rusted Medical Kit + 8× Antiseptic + 5× Surveyor Vault

Explosives Station

Grenades and explosive devices

  • Level 1: Gas grenade, light impact grenade
  • Level 2: Blaze grenade, shrapnel grenade, snap blast grenade
  • Level 3: Heavy fuze grenade, explosive mine, wolfpack
  • Upgrade: 50× Chemicals + 6× ARC Alloy → 3× Synthesized Fuel + 5× Crude Explosives + 5× Pop Trigger → 3× Laboratory Reagents + 5× Explosive Compound + 3× Rocketeer Driver

Utility Station

Gadgets and utility items

  • Level 1: Barricade kit, binoculars, door blocker, smoke grenade, remote raider flare
  • Level 2: Lure grenade, raider hatch key, zipline
  • Level 3: Photoelectric cloak, snap hook, tagging grenade
  • Upgrade: 50× Plastic + 6× ARC Alloy → 2× Damaged Heat Sink + 5× Electrical + 6× Snitch Scanner → 3× Fried Motherboard + 5× Advanced Electrical + 4× Leaper Pulse Unit

Refiner

Convert materials into refined components

  • Level 1: Crude explosives, durable cloth, electrical/mechanical components
  • Level 2: Advanced components, antiseptic, ARC circuitry, gun parts, mod components
  • Level 3: Magnetic accelerator, power rod
  • Upgrade: 60× Metal + 5× ARC Powercell → 3× Toaster + 5× ARC Motion Core + 8× Fireball Burner → 3× Motor + 10× ARC Circuitry + 6× Bombardier Cell

Scrappy (Passive)

Rooster that generates basic materials

  • Free: Generates Metal, Fabric, Plastic, Chemicals, Rubber, Seeds
  • Levels 1-5: Upgrade for more materials per raid
  • Max Hold: 50× each material type
  • Tip: Spend 15+ minutes in raid for maximum rewards

Pro Looting & Crafting Tips

Prioritize Keys First

Keys like Dam Control Tower or Stella Montis Archives unlock high-tier loot rooms with blueprints and rare materials.

Recycle Smart

Recycle items in Speranza for full materials. In-raid salvaging gives fewer resources but works in emergencies.

Extract Blueprints

Put blueprints in your Safe Pocket to guarantee extraction. They're one-time unlocks needed for workshop crafting.

Upgrade Scrappy

Level up Scrappy for passive materials. Spend 15+ minutes in raid for maximum rewards - even if you don't extract!

Workshop Upgrade Path

Focus on Refiner first to craft advanced components, then Gunsmith for weapons, then Gear Bench for augments.

Farm ARC Components

Kill specific ARCs for their parts: Wasp/Hornet Drivers, Bastion Cells, Sentinel Cores are needed for upgrades.

Team Coordination

Share materials and keys with squad. One person with Looting Mk. 3 augment benefits the whole team.

Balance Risk vs Reward

Locked rooms have the best loot but attract attention. Time your entry when ARCs are distracted or eliminated.
